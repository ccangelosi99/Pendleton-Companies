Your Growth Path

Career Development

We believe in promoting from within and rewarding dedication with real career growth and professional development opportunities.

Tenure Recognition

Rewarding Loyalty

At Pendleton, we believe that experience and institutional knowledge are invaluable. Our tenure recognition program acknowledges team members who invest their careers with us.

1 Year

Formal performance review, PTO tier advancement eligibility, 401(k) matching vesting begins

3 Years

Enhanced PTO, 50% 401(k) vesting, recognition bonus, priority access to internal transfer opportunities

5 Years

Full 401(k) vesting, senior tier benefits, tenure bonus, executive mentorship program access

10+ Years

Legacy recognition award, maximum PTO accrual (22 days), profit-sharing consideration, sabbatical eligibility

Promotions

Merit-Based Growth

Our promotion philosophy is simple: performance drives advancement. We maintain transparent career ladders across all divisions and actively develop internal candidates for leadership roles.

Annual Performance Reviews

Structured evaluations with clear metrics, 360-degree feedback, and documented development plans that create a roadmap for advancement.

Leadership Development Program

High-potential employees are identified and enrolled in our structured leadership program, featuring executive coaching, cross-divisional exposure, and project leadership opportunities.

Promote-From-Within Policy

All open positions are posted internally for two weeks before external recruitment begins. Internal candidates receive priority consideration and dedicated interview coaching.
