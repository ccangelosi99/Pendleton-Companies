Private Credit Overview

Specialized Lending Expertise

Pendleton's private credit strategy focuses on direct lending and mezzanine financing opportunities in the real estate and specialty sectors. Our experienced credit team evaluates lending opportunities with disciplined underwriting, strong sponsor relationships, and conservative loan structures. Private credit represents a growing component of our diversified investment portfolio, providing attractive risk-adjusted returns.
